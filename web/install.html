Hollowclock Firmware Installer v

With this tool you can load the firmware directly onto your Wemos D1 Mini without installing PlatformIO or the Arduino IDE.

Instructions:

  1. Connect your Wemos D1 Mini to your computer's USB port
  2. Click the "Install" button below
  3. Follow the instructions in the dialog
  4. After successful installation, the clock is ready to use
Note: This installer requires a modern browser that supports WebUSB (Chrome, Edge, or Opera). Firefox does not support WebUSB at this time.

After Installation:

After installation, the clock will immediately create a WiFi access point named "Hollowclock-XXXXXX" with password "12345678".

Connect to this network, and a configuration portal will automatically open. If it doesn't open automatically, you can access it at http://192.168.4.1

There you can select your WiFi network and the clock will connect to your home network.

Tip: The improved firmware now starts the WiFi setup immediately after installation without any delay!
